Generate tables of modular arithmetic with shading of absolute values. Scrolling and zooming for higher inputs can crash. Hold ctrl and press - to zoom out before you generate a value over 75, as large values are slow using Chrome, yet seamless on my phone with DuckDuckGo. The format is column modulo row. 50 is a good starting point, 100 is my sweet spot, and 150 is appealing if you are careful with your mouse and keyboard. Lower values have similar appeal.
